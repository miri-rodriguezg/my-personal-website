Languages

I grew up with a bilingual education and graduated school with both Spanish and English high school diplomas and proficiency in both languages.

I learnt French as an extracurricular and attending summer camps, achieving the equivalent of a B2 level.

I did a one month exchange program in Germany and continued self-teaching the language, receiving the equivalent of an A2.

Van Life & Camping

Spent weeks living in my 1997 old car, roadtripping across all the US West Coast and National Parks.

I love being in contact with nature and try to go camping once a year where I get to go on hikes, sleep under the stars, swim in lakes and stay in the wilderness.

CouchSurfing Community

I am a member of the couchsurfing community where I host and go to other people's houses for free with the sole purposes of making travelling easier and cheaper for people who share this passion.

I got to meet people from all over the world and hear about their cultures, travelling experiences and create new ones by going on hikes or showing eachother around our cities.
